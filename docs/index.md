## What is Trade Dialer?

Trade Dialer helps you track when stock markets around the world open and close. Whether you're trading US stocks, following European indices, or watching Asian markets, Trade Dialer shows you:

- **Live market status** — See which markets are open right now
- **Real-time countdowns** — Know exactly when a market opens or closes
- **Index values** — Track major indices like S&P 500, DAX, Nikkei 225 (updated every 5 minutes)
- **Holiday calendars** — Never be surprised by a closed market

## Supported Markets

Trade Dialer tracks **36 indices** across **17+ exchanges** worldwide:

### Americas
- **USA:** S&P 500, Dow Jones, NASDAQ-100
- **Canada:** S&P/TSX Composite, S&P/TSX 60
- **Mexico:** IPC
- **Brazil:** IBOVESPA

### Europe
- **UK:** FTSE 100, FTSE 250
- **Germany:** DAX, MDAX
- **France:** CAC 40
- **Netherlands:** AEX
- **Spain:** IBEX 35
- **Italy:** FTSE MIB
- **Switzerland:** SMI, SMIM
- **Turkey:** BIST 100

### Asia-Pacific
- **Japan:** Nikkei 225, TOPIX
- **China:** SSE Composite, SZSE Component
- **Hong Kong:** Hang Seng, HSCEI
- **South Korea:** KOSPI, KOSDAQ
- **Taiwan:** TAIEX
- **India:** NIFTY 50, SENSEX
- **Singapore:** STI
- **Saudi Arabia:** TASI

### Oceania & Africa
- **Australia:** ASX 200
- **South Africa:** JSE All Share

## Quick Start

1. **Visit** [trade-dialer.com](https://www.trade-dialer.com)
2. **See at a glance** which markets are open (green), closing soon (amber), or closed (gray)
3. **Check countdowns** to know when your market opens or closes
4. **View index values** updated every 5 minutes during trading hours
